Our Future of Work Initiative

Utilizing Big Data & Machine Learning to understand the regional Labour Market

We collect real-time information from more than 100 regional online Job posting platforms. With millions of job Posting observations, we can understand the region's labor market demand and track the relevance of skills and employment prospects.

Data & methodology

Explore the online job market

We use a custom-built data platform to analyze the online job market. Our data platform contains a mix of technologies that work in tandem to collect, clean, standardize, and analyze data.

Integration & API

Integrate our findings in your reporting in real-time

The Future of Work API services allows users to connect to our database and access the latest regional labor market data. The service offers skills and job aggregations in a timely fashion enabling users to utilize our Big Data and Machine Learning resources to understand the job market..
